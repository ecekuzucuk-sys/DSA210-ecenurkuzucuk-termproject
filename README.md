# DSA210 - Term Project

## Urban Behavior Analysis Across Cities

This project analyzes how environmental and social factors influence human mobility patterns across different cities.

---

## Cities

* Istanbul
* Ankara
* London

---

## Data Sources

* Google Community Mobility Reports
* Weather data (Open-Meteo)
* Air quality data (PM2.5)

---

## Research Questions / Hypotheses

* H1: Higher air pollution (PM2.5) decreases mobility.
* H2: Weather conditions (temperature, precipitation) affect mobility patterns.
* H3: Mobility patterns differ between cities within the same country.
* H4: Mobility patterns differ across countries.

---

## Methodology

* Data collection from multiple sources
* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* City-level aggregation (mean values)
* Visualization of mobility, air quality, and temperature across cities

---

## April 14 Progress (Milestone 1)

* Data collection completed
* Initial data cleaning performed
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) conducted
* Comparison of cities using visualization
* Combined analysis of mobility, PM2.5, and temperature

---

## Key Findings (Preliminary)

* Mobility levels vary across cities
* Istanbul shows higher air pollution levels compared to others
* Environmental factors may influence mobility patterns
* Cross-city differences indicate structural and environmental impacts
